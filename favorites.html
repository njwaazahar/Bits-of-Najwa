Books, Movies and Songs Chart!

1st Place Book:
Nirnama

Source: Goodreads


This is my all-time favorite novel! Nirnama's stories often involve themes of fighting against oppression and helping the marginalized, drawing inspiration from the rich folklore and legends of the Malay Nusantara. It is such a fun read!

Click Nirnama to own this beautiful writings!

1st Place Movie:
The Princess Diaries

Source: Rotten Tomatoes


This movie is light and easy-going. I can rewatch it multiple times! The plot follows Mia Thermopolis, a shy American teenager who learns she is heir to the throne of a European kingdom. Under the tutelage of her estranged grandmother, the kingdom's reigning queen, Mia must choose whether to claim or renounce her title.

Click The Princess Diaries to watch the classic trailer!

1st Place Song:
Love Maze

Source: Genius


"Love Maze" is a song by BTS. It was released on May 18, 2018, and appears as the sixth track for their third studio album Love Yourself: Tear. This song gives calming vibes and such an easy-listening. This song should be more recognizable!

Click Love Maze to listen to this bop!


2nd Place Book:
Bayang Sofea

Source: Goodreads


Finished it in just a few days! It tells the story of Sofea, a hardworking and ambitious woman, who dreams of success at RICE company. However, a chance encounter with Danny, a renowned boxer, brings back a forgotten past involving a mysterious organization that helped Sofea achieve her goals, but at a great cost. There are some heavy plots but still enjoyable!

Click Teori Pengakhiran Bayang Sofea to read 4 different ending theories! "You may choose your own ending!" - Teme Abdullah, author of Bayang Sofea

2nd Place Movie:
Pitch Perfect

Source: Rotten Tomatoes


This movie is so fun and funny. The amount of me rewatching it is uncountable! College student Beca knows she does not want to be part of a clique, but that's exactly where she finds herself after arriving at her new school. Thrust in among mean gals, nice gals and just plain weird gals, Beca finds that the only thing they have in common is how well they sing together. She takes the women of the group out of their comfort zone of traditional arrangements and into a world of amazing harmonic combinations in a fight to the top of college music competitions.

Click Pitch Perfect: The Finals to watch their iconic performance!

2nd Place Song:
Still With You

Source: Genius


Released as a part of the BTS' anniversary celebration known as Festa, over a jazzy R&B instrumental, the song reminisces missing someone and believing in being together. This is one of the best songs by Jung Kook. Everyone will fall in love with it at the first listening!

Click Still With You to listen to this special song by JK on the og platform, SoundCloud!


3rd Place Book:
Pada Subuh yang Membawaku Pergi

Source: Goodreads


This novel made me cry at 3am! Iskandar Dzulkarnain was abused since childhood by his father and lovingly caressed by his mother. Maturity brought him to his senses simply because he did not want his body to continue to be tortured. Family, friendship and love are expected to be constant, but the nature of life is that nothing lasts forever. It is heart-breaking novel, but in a good way.

Click Pada Subuh yang Membawaku Pergi to purchase it, and let's cry together!

3rd Place Movie:
Now You See Me 2

Source: IMDb


This movie is really different from others. They managed to amaze me with their magic tricks! It is the sequel to Now You See Me (2013) and the second installment in the Now You See Me film series. The plot follows the Four Horsemen, now led by FBI agent Dylan Rhodes, as they are coerced by tech magnate Walter Mabry into stealing a powerful data chip.

Click Now You See Me 2: Card Scene to watch a snippet of their iconic card tricks!

3rd Place Song:
Good Graces

Source: Genius


“Good Graces”, the third track on Short n' Sweet, is a confident and assertive song about setting boundaries in a relationship. The lyrics convey a message that while the singer can be sweet and loving, she won’t tolerate any disrespect or shady behavior. Despite not being the title track of the album, this song managed to be on the top!

Click Good Graces to sing-along with the lyric video!
